Interaction Design Analysis

Comparing interaction design approaches between Liquid Entropy and 7 Poems

Selected web UIs:

  • Liquid Entropy - Mika Chernov & Sinjin Hawke
  • 7 Poems - Justine Nguyễn-Nguyễn & Tiger Dingsun

Information design:

Liquid Entrophy

Liquid Entropy presents its information through a visual dynamic interface that can respond and interact with users. The layout is quite fluid with elements that shift and transform based on the user's input to create a sense of immersion. The use of color and motion effects draws user's attention to the key features, enhancing overall experience.

The designer's choice for this website might be focusing on creating a captivating environment that reflects the fluidity of sound and music, which further encourage exploration and interaction from the users.

7 Poems

7 Poems employs a minimalist design that emphasizes the text and audio elements.

Liquid Entropy has a dynamic and immersive design that focus on creating an environment with the fluidity of sound, this contrasts with 7 Poems’ minimalist approach that prioritize users’ literary experience. However, both of them serve their content effectively.
