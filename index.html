Interaction Design Analysis

Selected web UIs:

  • Liquid Entropy - Mika Chernov & Sinjin Hawke
  • 7 Poems - Justine Nguyễn-Nguyễn & Tiger Dingsun

Information design:

Liquid Entropy

Liquid Entropy presents its information through a visual dynamic interface that can respond and interact with users. The layout is quite fluid with elements that shift and transform based on the user's input to create a sense of immersion. The use of color and motion effects draws user's attention to the key features, enhancing overall experience.

The designer's choice for this website might be focusing on creating a captivating environment that reflects the fluidity of sound and music, which further encourage exploration and interaction from the users.

7 Poems

7 Poems features a minimalist design that emphasizes text and audio elements. Each poem is presented in a clean layout allowing the users to focus on the content. Moreover, the interface is straightforward with clear navigation for users to access different poems easily, enhancing readability and engagement.

The designer's rationale behind this decision shows the intention to prioritize literary experience which allows the poem and sound to take the center stage without any unnecessary distractions.

Liquid Entropy has a dynamic and immersive design that focus on creating an environment with the fluidity of sound, this contrasts with 7 Poems’ minimalist approach that prioritize users’ literary experience. However, both of them serve their content effectively.

Mapping:

Liquid Entropy

This website utilizes a mapping system where user interaction cause a direct impact on the visuals and the auditory output. The interface responds to the movements and clicks of the mouse, creating firect correlation between user actions and the resulting changes in sound and visuals.

This approach enhances user engagement as they can experiment with various interaction methods to discover unique soundscapes.

7 Poems

The mapping is more straightforward with clear buttons that guides users through the content. Each poem is paired with an audio player that activates when selected; providing a direct and intuitive audio experience.

This designer's choice ensures users can easily navigate through the content without any confusions or frustration, and focus more on the poetry itself and the accompanying music.

Liquid Entropy's interactive mapping encourages exploration, while 7 Poems offers a more traditional navigation experience that focuses on content access.

Characterization:

Liquid Entropy

Liquid Entropy characterizes its elements through vibrant visuals and soundscapes that evoke a sense of fluidity and creativity. The interface feels alive, with animations and sound effects that enhance the overall experience to make it feel like a living artwork. The dynamic visuals and sounds set the expectation that the interface is interactive and responsive to encourage user exploration and experimentation. While the visual and auditory respond is an explanation of how user's action influence the environment; making it clear that the interactions will lead to changes in sound and visuals.

This characterization reflects the designers' intent to create an engaging and artistic environment that resonates with users on an emotional level.

7 Poems

The use of different tempo and audio reflects the tone and mood of each poem. The interface is designed to be clean and contemplative to allow users to immerse themselves in the literary experience. The minimalist design sets the expectation that the attention will be on the poem and audio, guiding users to focus on the content rather than the interface. Besides, the consistent layout and predictable navigation establish a norm for how users can expect to interact with the interface, this reduce cognitive load and enhance usability.

This design approach emphasizes the importance of the content and atmosphere, allowing the poem to be conveyed without overwhelming the users with visual distractions.

Liquid Entropy's vibrant and dynamic characterization contrasts with 7 Poems' calm and focused. However, both of them enhance their user experiences through setting clear expectations and providing context for interaction.

Feedback:

Liquid Entropy

It provides real-time feedback through visual changes and audio effects that respond to user interactions. Users receive instant auditory and visual cues as they interact with the interface and this immediate response reinforce their actions and encourage further exploration.

The feedback mechanism of this website enhances user engagement by making the overall experience feel interactive and responsive.

7 Poems

7 Poems provides feedback through visual cues and audio playback when a poem is selected. It provides clear user interface instructions to ensure that users understand the status of their interactions.

This intuitive feedback approach maintains clarity and focus, avoiding confusion while enjoying.

The immersive feedback contrasts with the intuitive feedback, while each serves the respective user experiences effectively.
